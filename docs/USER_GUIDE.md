# CS One CRM User Guide (Phase 7)

## Overview
Phase 7 adds the commercial operating layer on top of the Phase 6 CRM platform. Admins can now brand the product, track subscriptions and invoices, manage onboarding readiness, and validate deployment health.

## Main areas
- **Dashboard**: operational overview plus subscription and onboarding KPIs.
- **CRM Core**: clients, leads, deals, and tasks.
- **Communication**: email/SMS logs, templates, and notifications.
- **Documents**: upload and secured delivery.
- **Reports / Workflows / AI**: analytics, automations, and AI tools.
- **Branding Center**: application naming, accent color, support email, login headline, and footer message.
- **Subscription & Billing Center**: plan records, seat counts, status, renewals, and invoices.
- **Onboarding Center**: go-live checklist and launch readiness tracking.

## Branding Center
